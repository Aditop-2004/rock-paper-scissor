Rock Paper Scissors

A fun game made by Aditya Technologies

Rules

  • The game will consist of 5 rounds.
  • In each round,you will get 3 choices:
    1. Rock
    2. Paper
    3. Scissor
  • Rock will defeat scissors.
  • Scissor will defeat paper.
  • Paper will defeat Rock.
  • After every 5 rounds ,you will get to know the winner.Then you can again start the new game.
